Description

This birthday countdown web app lets a user save specific birthdates and lets the user be aware of upcoming birthdays by providing a countdown of days until the upcoming birthday.


Goals

We are working to improve the user interface and we also plan to add family/friends categorization or other custom groupings for each user, as well as an indication of urgency of upcoming dates. The indicator is planned to be a color gradient or a dynamic icon.

Our main concept may also be extended to the functionality of a general calendar that can be shared between users. An example case would be a family of users can share the same calendar of birthday countdowns so no one forgets to prepare for upcoming birthdays.
